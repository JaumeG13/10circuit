About Us

10 Circuit is the official Assetto Corsa website for 10 Controls

10 Controls was strictly an F1 league, but one day we decided to just run some practice sessions and quickly found there was an interest for Assetto Corsa in our community.

This lead us to organise our first event which was a 2.4h multi-class endurance race at Istanbul Park, which kickstarted this project.

And so, 10 Circuit was born, starting off Season 1 with an 8 race calendar consisting of 2 events for each of the 4 different motorsport disciplines.
